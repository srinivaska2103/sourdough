01

How the Brand Started

It began in a small home kitchen filled with scattered flour dust and curiosity. Fueled by a simple desire to recreate old-world texture profiles, we set out on an experimental journey into the demanding, rewarding art of high-hydration wild-yeast fermentation.

02

First Sourdough Experiments

Our early attempts were defined by flat bakes, unpredictable room variables, and dense crumbs. However, every failed loaf taught us crucial lessons about temperature adaptations, wet gluten structural behaviors, and the importance of maintaining an active wild leaven.

03

Passion for Natural Fermentation

We quickly fell in love with natural, slow processes. By moving away from quick commercial additives, we uncovered the incredible health benefits and deep flavor matrices that can only be unlocked through extended, patience-driven bulk fermentation.

04

Inspiration Behind the Company

Today, our mission extends beyond selling premium tools and organic ingredients. We are dedicated to building a vibrant global community where bakers can confidently share cross-sections, fine-tune hydration scales, and sustain the timeless art of handcrafted baking.
