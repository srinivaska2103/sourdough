Biological Timeline

The Sourdough Process

Five key stages of slow biological transformation required to produce an authentic, high-hydration open crumb loaf at home.

Stage 01

Feed the Starter

Activate your wild live yeast populations with organic stone-ground flour blends to establish high volume potential.

Stage 02

Mix the Dough

Combine water matrices and flours gently to initiate autolyse hydration, formatting strong wet gluten link foundations.

Stage 03

Ferment Slowly

Allow the wet batch to rise across deep ambient bulk timelines to develop natural enzyme flavor complexities.

Stage 04

Shape Carefully

Build outer skin tension using delicate tuck methods before placing dough sets inside cold canvas proofing baskets.

Stage 05

Bake Beautiful Bread

Score with razor lames and slide onto stone decks under steam to unlock high spring heights and deep blistered cuts.
